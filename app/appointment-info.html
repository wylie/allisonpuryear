I am currently an out-of-network provider for most insurance companies. This means that post-session I supply my clients with a receipt which contains all the necessary information for insurance companies. Clients then submit the receipt to their insurance companies for direct reimbursement.

To determine if you have mental health coverage, the first thing you should do is check with your insurance carrier. Check your coverage carefully and find the answers to the following questions:

  1. Do I have mental health benefits?
  2. Do I need pre-authorization?
  3. How much does my plan cover for an out-of-network provider? (this is usually a percentage)
  4. What is my deductible and has it been met?
  5. Is approval/referral required from my primary care physician?
