Clinical Supervision

Whether you are a new graduate or fully licensed, I would love the chance to help you feel more competent in your practice. Business Consultation

When I worked in agencies, I longed for the autonomy & financial compensation of private practice. I was intimidated by the business side of things- working with insurance, getting referrals and creating efficient systems. I read books; I had business consultations; I picked the brains of so many private practitioners. When I took the risk, I was rewarded beyond my expectations and have loved practice building ever since. Having built practices in 3 cities (Athens, GA, Seattle, WA ↦ Asheville), I am confident in my ability to help you do the same. I am passionate about helping clinicians feel safe enough to take the leap that leads to work/life balance, less burn out & more freedom. There are so many fantastic providers working too much for too little because of fear.
